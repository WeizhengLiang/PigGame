Game Rule 😍

1. You need to reach 20 pts before your opponent does.

2. There are only one way you can earn pts: roll the dice.

3. Each time you hit ROLL, you temporarily get the same amount of pts as the dice is shown except when you roll a 1.

4. There are two ways you pass the round to your opponent: 1. hit the HOLD button to aquire all your temporary pts. 2. Roll a 1 to lose all you temporary pts.
